OUR Competitions

PAPER PRESENTATION

Paper presentation is all about how you put your topic or present your topic in front of the audience.We will see the way you present your topic, the way you put your points, your presentation style, your language and how promptly and effectively you answer there questions. Team consists of 2 to 3 members. Registration fee per team is 200/-(INR)

POSTER PRESENTATION

This competition provides a chance to express the innovative ideas through posters. It is a way to communicate your research or your understanding of a topic in a short and concise format. Team consists of 2 members Registration fee per team is 100/-(INR)

TECHNICAL QUIZ

Willing to take challenges? We will give you a chance to stamp your authority as a tech-savvy of your college! Come and show your talent in quiz. Team consists of 4 members. Registration fee per team is 200/-(INR).

EXPO/LIVEMODEL

competition where contestants present their working projects/models that they have created. It allows students to compete in today's challenging world......
Team consists of 3 members. Registration fee per team is 300/-

CHIEF PATRON

Founder & Chairman, Vignan's Group of Institutions

Vignan's Institute of Engineering for Women is a premier educational institute established in the year 2008. The college has been contributing for the cause of Women's technical education in north coastal Andhra Pradesh unstintingly for the past 10 years, since its inception. It's vision is to provide technical education and act as a rostrum for scientific Research. It's mission is to provide quality academic and training activities aimed at promoting employability,entrepreneurship, leadership to the global technological development. In accordance to this vision, the institute is maintaining exemplary records in academics.
